# Chapter 1 Outline: The Robotic Nervous System

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the analogy between a robot's control system and a biological nervous system.
- Define what a Robot Operating System (ROS) is and is not.
- Appreciate the history and evolution from ROS 1 to ROS 2.
- Understand the goals and philosophy of ROS 2.

3.  **What is ROS?**: A "Plumbing" System for Robots

5.  **Core Philosophy**: Modularity, Community, and Open Source
